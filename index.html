BONGAIGAON NORMAL SCHOOL

The Bongaigaon Normal School at Bongaigaon provides D. El. Ed. course, which has been vital in developing teaching proficiency. It is an NCTE recognized Teachers' training institution. It was established in 1967. It is an NCTE recognized institute under SCERT.

Course We Offer

We offer the best D. El. Ed. course for teaching aspirants!

D. El. Ed. (Diploma in Elementary Education)

The D. El. Ed. course is a full time 2 years' course (four semesters), Diploma in Elementary Education. The Diploma course is a crucial program designed for individuals aspiring to become effective teachers at the elementary level. The seat capacity is 50 trainees per year.
